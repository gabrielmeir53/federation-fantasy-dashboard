Preamble

We, the members of the Fantasy Sports Federation, establish this Constitution to govern the operation of our multi-sport dynasty fantasy league. The Federation operates four concurrent leagues across the NFL, NBA, NHL, and MLB, with the goal of crowning an overall champion based on combined performance across all sports.

Article I — Federation Structure

Section A: Leagues

The Federation shall consist of four (4) leagues, each corresponding to a major North American professional sport:

  1. Federation Football League (FFL) — NFL
  2. Federation Basketball Association (FBA) — NBA
  3. Federation Hockey League (FHL) — NHL
  4. Federation Baseball League (FBL) — MLB

All leagues are hosted on Fantrax and must be set to Public visibility.

Section B: Teams & Ownership

The Federation consists of twelve (12) member teams. Each owner operates a single franchise that competes in all four leagues simultaneously.

Section B: Tiebreakers

In the event of a tie in Federation Points, the following tiebreakers shall be applied in order:

  1. Most league championships (1st-place finishes)
  2. Highest combined win percentage across all leagues
  3. Highest average points per game
  4. Head-to-head record

Article III — Season Format

Section A: Regular Season

Each league follows its respective sport's standard fantasy season schedule as configured on Fantrax. The regular season length varies by sport:

  • NFL: ~14 weeks
  • NBA: ~22 weeks
  • NHL: ~24 weeks
  • MLB: ~24 weeks

Customize week counts to match your league's configuration.

Section B: Playoffs

Each league shall have a 6-team playoff format:

  • Top 6 teams qualify based on regular season standings
  • Seeds 1 and 2 receive a first-round bye
  • Quarterfinals: Seed 3 vs 6, Seed 4 vs 5
  • Semifinals: Seed 1 vs lowest remaining, Seed 2 vs other
  • Championship: Semifinal winners
  • Consolation rounds determine 3rd through 6th place

Article IV — Transactions

Article VI — Governance

The Federation shall be governed by a Commission of designated members with equal voting power. The Commission is responsible for:

  • Interpreting and enforcing the Constitution
  • Resolving disputes between owners
  • Managing league settings on Fantrax
  • Approving or vetoing trades when necessary
  • Maintaining the dashboard and scraper infrastructure

Article VII — Amendments

This Constitution may be amended by a two-thirds (2/3) supermajority vote of all member teams. Proposed amendments must be submitted in writing and discussed for a minimum of 48 hours before a vote is called.

Types of legislative action:

  • Commission Directive: An administrative action taken by the Commission that does not require a vote (e.g., draft lottery results, platform selection).
  • Commission Resolution: A proposal by the Commission requiring a vote of all member teams.
  • Federation Resolution: A proposal by a member team (or group of teams) requiring a vote to amend the Constitution.
  • Commission Notice: An official announcement or clarification from the Commission.

Article VIII — Code of Conduct

The Federation hereby adopts this Code of Conduct as an official and binding document. All owners, by virtue of their membership, agree to abide by the principles and rules outlined below.

Principles of Conduct

A. Sportsmanship and Respect — All owners shall conduct themselves respectfully in all official and unofficial league communications. Harassment, personal attacks, discrimination, and the use of hateful or offensive language will not be tolerated. Friendly rivalries and competitive banter are encouraged but must not cross the line into malicious personal insults or targeted harassment.

B. Competitive Integrity — All owners have a duty to compete. Collusion, tanking, and bad-faith transactions are strictly prohibited.

C. League Activity and Engagement — All owners are expected to actively manage their rosters, communicate in a timely manner, and fulfill their financial responsibilities to the Federation.

Enforcement and Penalties

Any owner who believes a violation has occurred may bring the issue to the Commission for review. The Commission will investigate all credible reports and has final authority to interpret this Code of Conduct.

Minor Infractions: An informal or formal warning from the Commission.

Significant Infractions: Reversal of a transaction, forfeiture of a game, temporary suspension of roster transaction privileges, or forfeiture of FAAB.

Major or Repeated Infractions: Forfeiture of future draft picks, suspension of league activities for a set period, or, in the most severe cases, suspension or expulsion from the Federation.

Comm. Notice IV — Trade Procedures

Teams must follow these procedures to submit and process a trade:

  1. Once teams have come to an agreement, the proposing team shall create a group chat with the accepting team owner(s) and at least one member of the Commission, with a message stating the terms of the trade.
  2. The accepting team owner(s) must confirm the terms prior to the Commission informing the Federation. The 48-hour review period will not begin until confirmation of acceptance by all teams involved.
  3. Once confirmed, a Commission member will send a message to the Federation informing them of the trade and its terms.
  4. A team may submit a complaint to any member of the Commission, who will then present the complaint to the rest of the Commission and begin the review process outlined in the Constitution.
  5. As soon as they are able to, the Commission shall enter the trades on Fantrax, setting the processing time 48 hours from when the trade was accepted by all parties.
